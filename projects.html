Online Car Service Booking

The purpose of this project is to develop an efficient and user-friendly online car servicing system that addresses the limitations of traditional service centre management. Unlike existing systems, this solution ensures greater accessibility and convenience for customers. Through this platform, customers can easily book their vehicles for service, record their complaints, and even choose their preferred mechanic. Additionally, they can track the status of their service in real time, including details of the mechanic performing the service and the individual responsible for delivering the vehicle back to their home. A key feature of the system is the ability for customers to access their complete service history, which adds value when maintaining their vehicles and can also serve as a useful reference during resale. Overall, the system enhances transparency, customer satisfaction, and efficiency in the vehicle servicing process.

SmartSole

SmartSole is an innovative AI-powered shoe website designed to revolutionize the way people discover, customize, and purchase footwear. Leveraging the latest advancements in artificial intelligence and data analytics, SmartSole provides personalized shoe recommendations based on user preferences, activity levels, and foot measurements. The platform offers a seamless online experience with smart features like virtual try-ons, real-time fitting suggestions, and adaptive style matching. By combining cutting-edge technology with fashion-forward design, SmartSole aims to deliver comfort, style, and precision tailored to every step.
